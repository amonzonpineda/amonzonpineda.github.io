6 Core Strategies

A Framework Built for Execution

Six sequential strategies that take a team from relationship-building all the way to sustained business alignment.

01
Connect
Onboarding Discovery sessions and Motivation Drive conversations build the personal rapport that makes coaching land and resistance dissolve.
02
Analyze
Data-driven Root Cause Analysis to identify metric gaps, trends, and behavioral drivers, replacing assumptions with evidence.
03
Standardize
Clarity Sessions establish shared expectations for every metric, behavior, and process, eliminating ambiguity before performance conversations begin.
04
Develop
Targeted coaching using Discovery Sessions and Skill Practice Labs to close gaps through observation-based feedback and guided role-play.
05
Follow Up
Structured reinforcement conversations (Recognition, Growth and Corrective) that lock in behaviors and close accountability loops.
06
Align
Monthly Performance Retrospectives and Goal Setting sessions sync leadership objectives across all levels, from senior leader to frontline agent.

Core Methodology

The Science Behind the Framework

Every decision in the framework is backed by proven methodologies used across high-performing operations globally.

Data and Analysis
  • SMART goal framework integrated at every leadership level
  • 5-Why root cause technique applied to behavior and metric gaps
  • Primary and secondary metric cross-analysis informs coaching plans
  • Six Sigma principles applied to identify process variation, reduce performance defects, and drive measurable team improvement
Decision Making
  • Skill vs. Will matrix guides coaching strategy selection for each agent
  • Effort-Impact quadrant prioritizes high-leverage coaching actions
  • Scope analysis determines whether gaps are individual or systemic
Coaching Culture
  • Observation-based feedback culture replacing assumptions with evidence
  • Documentation at every stage drives accountability for leaders and agents
  • Self-discovery questioning shifts ownership to the employee
